The Best Movies According to Joshua

My top 3 movies of all time

The Dark Knight

I love Batman Movies, but the depiction of the Joker in this movie, along with seeing Batman moral values pushed, was juts amazing and thrilling.

Ice Age

There are many great animated movies out there, but this one gives me the most nostalgia with memories from my childhood with my little brother.

The Godfather

The best movie about a mob in the world, there is nothing to be said, just watch it.
